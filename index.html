About Monstera Deliciosa

  • Light: Bright, indirect
  • Water: When top inch of soil is dry
  • Pet Safe: No

Monstera deliciosa, also known as the Swiss cheese plant or philodendron, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central America. It's popular as a houseplant due to its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves with distinctive natural holes and splits. The plant can grow quite large indoors and is appreciated for its striking appearance and air-purifying qualities. In the wild, it can produce a fruit that’s edible when ripe, tasting like a mix of pineapple and banana—hence the name deliciosa.

About Fiddle Leaf Fig

  • Light: Bright, filtered
  • Water: When top 1-2 inches of soil are dry
  • Pet Safe: No

Ficus lyrata, commonly known as the fiddle leaf fig, is a popular houseplant known for its large, violin-shaped leaves. Native to the tropical rainforests of West Africa, it thrives in bright, indirect light and requires regular watering to maintain its lush foliage. The fiddle leaf fig has become a favorite among interior designers for its dramatic presence and ability to enhance any space with its bold, sculptural form.

About Pilea

  • Light: Bright, indirect
  • Water: Allow soil to dry before waterings
  • Pet Safe: Yes

Pilea peperomioides, commonly known as the Chinese money plant or friendship plant, is a popular houseplant known for its unique round leaves and easy care. Native to the Yunnan province of China, it has gained popularity worldwide for its charming appearance and ability to thrive in various indoor conditions. The plant is often propagated through offsets, making it a great gift among friends, hence the name "friendship plant."

About Golden Pothos

  • Light: Low to bright, indirect
  • Water: When soil is dry
  • Pet Safe: No

Epipremnum aureum, commonly known as golden pothos or devil's ivy, is a hardy and versatile houseplant known for its trailing vines and heart-shaped leaves. Native to the Solomon Islands, it thrives in a variety of indoor conditions, making it a popular choice for both beginners and experienced plant enthusiasts. The golden pothos is also known for its air-purifying qualities, helping to improve indoor air quality while adding a touch of greenery to any space.

About Peace Lily

  • Light: Low to medium, indirect
  • Water: Keep soil moist, not soggy
  • Pet Safe: No

Spathiphyllum, commonly known as the peace lily, is a popular houseplant known for its elegant white flowers and glossy green leaves. Native to tropical regions of the Americas, it thrives in low light and is relatively easy to care for, making it a favorite among indoor gardeners. The peace lily is also known for its air-purifying properties, helping to remove toxins from the air while adding a touch of beauty to any space.
